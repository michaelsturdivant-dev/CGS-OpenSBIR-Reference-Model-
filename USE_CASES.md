# Use Cases

## 1. SBIR/STTR Readiness

A small business has a technical idea but lacks a structured problem statement, commercialization pathway, work plan, data-rights boundary, and execution tracker.

CGS Sentinel OpenSBIR™ provides a reference workflow for organizing the effort and preparing the buyer for a paid readiness sprint.

## 2. OTA Concept Packaging

A contractor or innovation team needs to convert a prototype concept into a defensible OTA pathway with stakeholders, milestones, evidence, and transition logic.

The model supports concept intake, opportunity tracking, work-item structure, and artifact planning.

## 3. Grant Operations

A nonprofit, church, clinic, or community coalition is pursuing multiple grants but has scattered deadlines, unclear ownership, incomplete attachments, and no execution rhythm.

The model creates a repeatable operating structure for tracking opportunities, tasks, artifacts, and dashboard summaries.

## 4. Community Infrastructure Pilot

A city, church network, or nonprofit coalition is coordinating food access, childcare, transportation, EV charging, workforce training, healthcare access, and housing pathways.

The model provides a way to organize complex ecosystem projects into lanes, work items, and funding pathways.

## 5. Healthcare Access Coordination

A clinic or wellness provider needs to coordinate credentialing, partner intake, workflow design, payer readiness, compliance boundaries, and implementation steps.

The model supports structured intake and operational task tracking without placing private healthcare data in the public repository.

## 6. Strategic Planning Committee Operations

A board, church, nonprofit, or coalition needs charters, subcommittees, agendas, action tracking, and implementation discipline.

The model supports committee workflow design, task ownership, and artifact planning.

## 7. Government Contracting Support

A contractor needs to organize PWS, SOW, POAM, travel, policy, compliance, force structure, acquisition, and program-management requirements.

The model provides the public reference structure. CGS can provide paid configuration and implementation support.
